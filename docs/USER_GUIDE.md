## ğŸ—ºï¸ Field Mapping

Map your farm fields using GPS.

### Why Map Your Fields?

- Get more accurate crop recommendations
- Track field sizes in hectares/acres
- Record soil types for each field
- Better yield predictions

### Mapping a New Field

1. From Home, open **Field Mapping**
2. Tap **Start Mapping**
3. Enter a **Field Name** (e.g., "North Plot")
4. Select **Soil Type**
5. Walk to the first corner of your field
6. Tap **Add Point**
7. Walk to each corner, adding points
8. Add at least 3 points (more is better)
9. Tap **Save Field**

### During Mapping

- **Current Location**: Shows your GPS position
- **Points Added**: Shows how many corners marked
- **Area**: Automatically calculates as you go
- **Units**: Shows hectares and acres

### Tips for Accurate Mapping

âœ… **Do:**
- Walk the actual boundary
- Wait for GPS to stabilize at each point
- Add points at every corner/curve
- Map on a clear day

âŒ **Don't:**
- Map during heavy rain
- Rush between points
- Forget to save your field

### Managing Fields

- View all saved fields on the main screen
- See area and soil type for each
- Delete fields you no longer need
- Use fields when creating crop plans

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the email instructions.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Some features require internet. Basic viewing works offline, but AI features need connection.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support or use Privacy Settings to request account deletion.

### Crop Recommendations

**Q: Why don't I see any crop recommendations?**
A: Make sure GPS is enabled and you have internet connection.

**Q: The recommendations don't match my area. Why?**
A: Ensure GPS accuracy is high. Try moving outdoors for better signal.

**Q: Can I get recommendations for crops not in Ethiopia?**
A: The app is optimized for Ethiopian crops and conditions.

### Disease Detection

**Q: The disease detection is wrong. What should I do?**
A: AI is not 100% accurate. For serious issues, consult a local agricultural expert.

**Q: What plants can I scan?**
A: The app recognizes most common Ethiopian crops and vegetables.

### Field Mapping

**Q: My field size seems wrong. Why?**
A: GPS accuracy varies. For precise measurements, use a professional surveyor.

**Q: Can I edit a saved field?**
A: Currently, you need to delete and re-map. Editing feature coming soon.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app is slow. How can I fix it?**
A: Clear app cache, restart the app, or check your internet connection.

**Q: I get "Permission Denied" errors. Why?**
A: Make sure you're logged in. Try logging out and back in.

**Q: The AI isn't responding. What's wrong?**
A: Check internet connection. The AI service may be temporarily busy.
